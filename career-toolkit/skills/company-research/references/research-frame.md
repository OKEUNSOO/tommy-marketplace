# Company Research Frame

Use this frame when live research is unavailable or when turning research into application strategy.

## 1. Company Problem

- What problem does the company solve?
- Who is the customer or user?
- What market or operational pressure is visible from the JD?

## 2. Business Model

- How does the company likely make money?
- What behavior or operation drives revenue?
- What data would the team care about?

## 3. Role Mission

- What work will this role probably own?
- Which responsibilities are immediate execution tasks?
- Which requirements reveal hidden team pain?

## 4. KPI Guess

- Acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, cost, quality, risk, automation, or operational efficiency.
- Mark guesses as `추정` unless directly sourced.

## 5. Experience Connection

- Match only projects backed by source career notes.
- Prefer problem similarity over tool-name similarity.
- Identify missing proof or weak fit honestly.

## 6. Application Angle

- 지원동기 should mention a specific company task or product context.
- Then connect one or two relevant experiences.
- End with a realistic contribution.
